How to help Pandas?

One of the key measures to restore the panda population is to protect their natural habitat. This includes creating sanctuaries and national parks where pandas can live without harm from humans.

We have little time!

The animals are in danger of extinction due to massive deforestation of bamboo forests, their natural habitat. In addition, pandas do not reproduce well, both in zoos and in the wild.
